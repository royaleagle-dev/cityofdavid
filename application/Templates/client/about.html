About the Ministry

The goal of the City of David Mount of Zion Ministries is to reach the nations of the world with Good News of Jesus Christ through preaching the true word of God with the Holy Bible and getting millions of people ready for the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, of the theme No one Left Behind, First-Class Rapture. Prayer is key, so come and let us all allow the Holy Spirit to give us the directions in life. As we all fellowship together, May God always be in our midst.

Pastor Mrs. Esther Aderinkola

Lead Pastor

Pastor Mrs. Esther Aderinkola graduated from Strayer University with an Associate Degree and her Bachelor of Science in Computer Information System. And then later achieved her Master of Science Degree in Management Information System at Bowie State University. Pastor Esther is married with four children and one grandson and loves their support and encouragements.
